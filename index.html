Help

How do I make a new document? => Click the New button or press N.

How do I undo a step? => Click the Undo button or press Z.

How do I redo a step? => Click the Redo button or press R.

How do I delete a document? => Click the Delete button or press W.

How do I export into image? => Click the Export button or press S. Then right-click the image and select Save Image As.

How do I edit the title of a document? => Double-click the title or press E.

How do I save a document? => OhBoard saves everything automatically for you!

How do I switch between documents? => Click the title of the document or press Up/Down arrow.

How do I switch between pencil & eraser? => Click the icons or press Left/Right arrow.

How do I update OhBoard? => Chrome > Window > Extensions > Developer mode > Update extensions now. Details.

Where should I contact for bug report/feedback? => Email to stephen@ohboard.com, or use this contact form.

A list of shortcuts:

  • W: Delete document

  • Z: Undo
  • R: Redo
  • E: Edit title
  • Up Arrow: Previous document
  • Down Arrow: Next document
  • Left/Right Arrow: change between pencil/eraser
  • Shift + Drag: make straight line
